Wait, what is this?

"The idea behind Micro Frontends is to think about a website or web app as a composition of features which are owned by independent teams. Each team has a distinct area of business or mission it cares about and specialises in. A team is cross functional and develops its features end-to-end, from database to user interface." (https://micro-frontends.org/)

This basic webshop is an attempt to use Micro Frontends as described by https://micro-frontends.org/. The cart, the product highlights and the user service are independently developed and deployed Micro Frontends, composed together by Tailor.js during run- not compiletime.

Give the following stand-alone applications a try: Cart (Widget), Login, Product Highlights

Have a look at my entry at Senacor's blog for a HowTo and more thoughts.
